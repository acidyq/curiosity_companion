The Sphinx is a Reptile
Well, a rep-tile, which isn’t quite the same thing. Short for
‘replicating tile’, this word refers to a shape that appears –
magnified – when several copies of it are put together. The most
obvious rep-tile is a square.




                                   Four square tiles make
                                   a bigger square.
