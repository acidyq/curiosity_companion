Numerical Spell

following trick. Select any number on the board shown below.
Spell it out, letter by letter. Add together the corresponding
numbers (subtracting those on black squares, adding those on
white squares). The result will always be plus or minus the
number you chose. For instance, TWENTY-TWO leads to
